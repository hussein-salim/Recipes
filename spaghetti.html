Description

This spaghetti is a classic Italian dish with a rich tomato sauce and perfectly cooked pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Saut√© onion and garlic in olive oil until soft.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Toss cooked spaghetti with sauce and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy.
