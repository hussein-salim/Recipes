Tacos

Home

Description

These tacos are filled with flavorful beef, fresh vegetables, and topped with a zesty salsa.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup salsa

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef with onion and garlic. Add taco seasoning and water. Simmer until thickened.
  2. Warm taco shells in the oven.
  3. Fill taco shells with beef mixture and top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and salsa.
  4. Serve and enjoy.
