Lasagna

Home

Description

This lasagna is a delicious, cheesy delight with layers of savory meat sauce and creamy ricotta cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 12 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook and drain noodles.
  3. Brown ground beef with onion and garlic.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese with egg, parsley, and salt.
  5. Layer the ingredients in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes.
  7. Let cool before serving.
